## 1. Purpose

Defines how NACE hierarchy is used for **sector aggregation and reporting**
in ESG, Risk, and Sustainability analytics.

---

## 2. Aggregation Levels

Supported levels:
- Section
- Division
- Group
- Class
- Activity

---

## 3. Reporting Rules

- Aggregation is always version-scoped
- Mixed-version aggregation is not permitted
- Hierarchy integrity is validated before reporting

---

## 4. ESG Use Cases

- Sector emission intensity
- Portfolio exposure
- Regulatory disclosure (e.g. EU taxonomy alignment)
